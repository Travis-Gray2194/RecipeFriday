Egg Sauce Recipe

Nigeria

Sweet and flavory Red braised Chicken wings. Easy to make and yummy enough for serving guests.

Recipe Directions

  1. Melt butter over low heat in a heavy saucepan.
  2. Stir in flour, increasing heat if necessary until the mixture gently bubbles.

Cook 2 minutes

  1. Remove pan from burner, add milk, stir and return to stove over medium high heat.
  2. Cook, stirring constanly, until mixture comes to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and cook 2 minutes, stirring constantly, adding more milk if the sauce becomes too thick.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, add chopped egg and mix.

Next:

  1. Heat a small amount of butter or olive oil in a skillet and saute the cakes, turning several times, until golden brown or about 8 minutes total cooking time.
  2. Serve at once with tartar sauce, mustard, or cocktail sauce on the side.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon white pepper
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
